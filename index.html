About Colourless

The game, Colourless, follows the adventure of a young man who is unable to see colour. Distraught by this, he travels the known world and discovers the world around him through finding colours. As more colours are discovered, the more the known world is discovered.

The Important Things

Key Features

Open World Design

The player can explore the world, as if they were actually there. The open world design allows for countless adventures to be had and many places to be discovered.

Combat

Combat within the world is next level. Enemies are present at every turn, challenging the player to become stronger in order to defeat them.

Achievements

There are over 50 achievements to unlock throughout the game ranging from assisting villagers to killing many enemies.

Lore

Extensive lore behind each character and opponent exists adding to the immersiveness of the game.
